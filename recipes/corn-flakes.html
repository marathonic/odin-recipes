Corn flakes

It's a classic

Ingredients

  • Corn flakes
  • Almond milk
  • Almond butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, drop a scoop of almond butter
  2. Pour some Corn Flakes. I love Kellogg's
  3. Pour cold almond milk until they cover the Corn Flakes
  4. Stir well until the milk is mixed with the almond butter.
  5. Enjoy!
