Curry

Description

I love Japanese curry. It's amazing! You're gonna love it!

Ingredients

  • Some vegetables you like and 2 potatoes
  • A couple of curry roux cubes
  • A sweetening agent, like maple, orange juice, etc.

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes and vegetables into chunks
  2. Add the vegetables to a hot, oiled pan, mix well
  3. Add water and bring to a boil
  4. Use a fine mesh strainer to get clearer broth
  5. Add the sweetening agent and simmer uncovered for 20 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes
  6. Add the potatoes and cook until tender, around 15 minutes
  7. Turn off the heat and take pot off the hub, add the curry roux
  8. The curry is ready! You can choose to serve with rice if you'd like!
